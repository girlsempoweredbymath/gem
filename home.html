ABOUT GIRLS EMPOWERED BY MATH (GEM)

Student-Run

GEM is by the students, for the students. Even our website is programmed by students! If you're a girl in the DMV area, please reach out to get involved. We would love to have you :) We are looking to implement school chapters.

Our Story

We noticed the gender disparity in STEM careers, math specifically. While you often see programs for STEM like Girls Who Code or Girls in Science, you don't usually see programs for girls in math. And we wanted to change that.

Our Services
